Our Certificates and Online Program For 2k23

By being the only private engineering college to get accreditation by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for 5 years (from 2009 to 2014), Chandigarh Engineering College (CEC) becomes an engineering institution holding high stature in the region. It has acclaimed a distinguished spot among the top-tier engineering colleges in India. With highly qualified and experienced faculty from IITs and other premier institutes, CEC has published 1200+ research papers and 150+ Patents. Keeping up with the current engineering trends and innovations, the college keeps abreast of the latest teaching pedagogy ensuring the students get the best of it. CEC is frontier in placements with an exclusive 360-degree training program. These have enabled the worthy students of CEC to get jobs in notable companies like Microsoft, Infosys, TCS, WIPRO, L&T, Samsung, Capgemini, Tech Mahindra, etc.


Being a progressive department, it aims at imparting the latest knowledge, pertinent to the above-mentioned domains, by putting the updated curriculum, research, and practice to use. There are different courses under the banner of CCT which concoct different ways of bettering life all over the world. Since Biotechnology is in its flying colors, CCT has taken exclusive care of this domain. Under great teaching staff and hard-working faculty members, the students get aligned with the international working paradigm which helps them throughout–from education to career.CCT taps into every aspect and that is why everything—from lectures to the industrial interface—has been meticulously interwoven.
